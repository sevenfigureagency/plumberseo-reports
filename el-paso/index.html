Why They're Winning

The companies at the top of the El Paso market didn't get there by accident. Here's what separates the dominant three from everyone else.

Reliable Plumbing and Plumbing Guys of El Paso maintain active profiles with photo galleries and up-to-date hours, signaling to Google that they're current operators. Most invisible competitors either don't have a GBP or haven't claimed/verified theirs.
Review Velocity
Mr. Rooter accumulates approximately 113 reviews per month based on its 1,359 total (assuming 12 months of active collection). Reliable Plumbing shows 526 reviews at high velocity. Neither company has stale reviews. Most invisible competitors haven't posted a review in months or years, which signals to Google they're inactive. Review recency is a ranking factor.
Service Pages
Mr. Rooter and Liberty Plumbing publish dedicated service pages for emergency plumbing, water heaters, drain cleaning, and commercial services. They're also ranking in organic results for these terms. Most competitors have generic "Services" pages with no unique content per service line.
Backlink Authority
Mr. Rooter benefits from the national Mr. Rooter brand network (hundreds of referring domains) plus local citations in BBB, Yelp, and Google My Business. Reliable Plumbing ranks for "plumber El Paso" organically, indicating stronger domain authority than most competitors. Invisible companies have minimal or no backlinks.
