What the Analysis Shows

FINDING 01
Reputation Doesn't Equal Visibility
E.R. Services has 4,696 five-star reviews (the highest in the market) but ranks only #12 in Maps. Benjamin Franklin Plumbing has 3,964 reviews but ranks #3. Review count alone doesn't drive ranking. Recency, velocity, and search signals matter more.
FINDING 02
Organic Search Is Nearly Uncontested
Only 4 of 18 companies appear in organic search results for tracked keywords. Queen City Plumbing (Organic #2), Charlotte Plumbing (Organic #6), Michael & Son Services (Organic #8), and Bliss Plumbing (Organic #4) own the entire organic space. Six companies with 1,000+ reviews don't appear once.
FINDING 03
Conversion Tools Are Missing
Only 5 of 18 companies offer online booking. Zero offer live chat or SMS. This means homeowners searching at 10pm can't book an appointment immediately. Companies that enable frictionless booking will capture leads that others lose.
FINDING 04
The Top 3 Are Pulling Away
Queen City Plumbing and Charlotte Plumbing appear #1 or #2 in Maps for nearly every tracked keyword. They're also the only top-5 companies with consistent organic visibility. The gap between them and the next tier is widening every month.
