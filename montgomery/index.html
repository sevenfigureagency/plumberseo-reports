Review Velocity

Service Pages
The top 3 companies rank for multiple service-specific keywords. They have dedicated content for emergency plumbing, water heater repair, drain cleaning. Most competitors rank for generic "plumber Montgomery" but not for the more specific, higher-intent terms where customers actually convert.
Backlink Authority
Dixie Electric, Edwards, and Roto Rooter have established local authority through citations, local directories, and backlinks. Roto Rooter's national brand adds institutional authority. Companies not ranking in organic have minimal backlink profiles and weak local citation presence.

Schitt's Plumbing has 676 five-star reviews and zero Maps visibility. Dixie Electric has 2,673 reviews and Maps #1. Review count alone doesn't drive ranking; velocity, recency, and GBP optimization matter more.
FINDING 02
The Top 3 Own the Market
Dixie Electric, Edwards, and Roto Rooter combined capture 68% of search visibility. That concentration is growing as they accumulate reviews faster than competitors. The market is consolidating, not diversifying.
