Review Velocity
1st Call Plumbing & AC has accumulated 1,965 five-star reviews. Chambliss Plumbing has 1,429 five-star reviews. These review profiles signal authority and trustworthiness to Google's algorithm. The companies adding the most reviews each month gain the most algorithmic advantage.
