What the Analysis Shows

FINDING 01
Perfect Reviews Don't Equal Visibility
Mother Modern Plumbing (5.0 stars, 654 reviews) and Drain Kings Plumbing (5.0 stars, 439 reviews) have the strongest reputations in the market. Yet they rank Maps #8 and don't appear in top 20 respectively. Reputation alone doesn't drive visibility — ranking infrastructure matters more.
FINDING 02
The Mid-Tier Trap
Seven companies rank Maps #5–#15 with strong reviews (4.6–5.0 stars) but capture only 25% of search visibility. They're stuck: too visible to ignore, too low to generate consistent volume. They're the perfect acquisition targets for dominant players.
FINDING 03
Invisible Giants
ABC Plumbing (854 reviews, 4.9 stars), Team Enoch (1,035 reviews, 4.9 stars), and Drain Kings Plumbing (439 reviews, 5.0 stars) have exceptional reputations but zero search visibility. They've built brand authority with zero search strategy. Their growth is capped by referral networks alone.
FINDING 04
The Organic Anomaly
Berkeys Plumbing ranks organic #1 for core terms but is absent from Maps entirely. Rockwater Plumbing ranks organic #8 but isn't in top 20 Maps. Both companies are splitting their presence between channels and losing overall market share as a result.
