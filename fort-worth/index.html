Section 07 — Key Findings

What the Data Tells Us

FINDING 01
Excellent reputation cannot compensate for digital invisibility
Trusty Plumber (5.0★, 39 reviews), 1-Tom-Plumber (5.0★, 305 reviews), and Rockwater Plumbing (5.0★, 120 reviews) hold perfect ratings — yet capture minimal digital traffic. A homeowner searching "plumber Fort Worth" at 10pm will never find them. Proof of quality is useless without visibility.
FINDING 02
The top 3 competitors have critical conversion infrastructure gaps
Benjamin Franklin (#1), ClearWater (#2), and Plumb Shield (#3) offer no online scheduling and no live chat. They are winning on visibility, but every operator who implements online booking and SMS response captures conversion share those three cannot defend.
FINDING 03
The Map Pack is the primary revenue mechanism in Fort Worth
Map Pack positions capture 60–70% of local search clicks. Companies ranked #1–3 in the Maps Pack generate disproportionate revenue. The data is clear: getting into the Pack is the primary revenue lever.
FINDING 04
Secondary terms remain genuinely open
Drain cleaning, water heater repair, emergency plumber, and sewer repair terms do not have entrenched leaders. Review velocity and GBP investment over 90 days can produce measurable shifts. That window will close as market consolidation accelerates.
